About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science, Stony Brook University, working under the supervision of Prof. Dimitris Samaras. I also work closely with Prof. David Gu on optimal transport. My research interests are machine learning and computer vision in general, including dictionary learning, deep generative models and their applications to image-to-image translation, image classification, image segmentation, and so on. Currently, I focus on optimal transport and its applications to Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs).
